Registration Process

If you would like to participate in SCMUN IX as a school or an individual delegate, please use the link below to register. For further questions, please email scmun@napchina.org. To retrieve a copy of our invitation letter, please click on the "Invitation Letter" button. After receiving your registration, we will be sending you the Registration Form II for delegate positions and logistic arrangements.
