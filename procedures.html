Ruels and Procedures

Overview

This is the New Guidelines For Delegates, which contains basic information and regulations for SCMUN VIII. The New Guidelines For Delegates will inform the required preparation before the conference and the permissive behaviors during the conference for delegates.

To be more specific, The New Guidelines For Delegates will guide you, including but not limited to:

  1. What the Different Positions At SCMUN Are
  2. How To Research A Country, An Issue
  3. How To Write A Policy Statement
  4. How To Write A Draft Resolution
  5. How To Write An Opening Speech
  6. How To Prepare Official Notepaper
  7. What Is Lobbying
  8. What Happen During Debate Time
